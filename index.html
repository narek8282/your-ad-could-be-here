The idea

A movie about advertising, made out of advertising.

Your Ad Could Be Here is an absurd anti-film. Instead of hiding sponsors inside a normal story, the project turns sponsors into the story itself: title cards, warnings, screen walls, credits, product shots, and paid messages.

Early brands can become part of the first version before the project opens wider in the US.
