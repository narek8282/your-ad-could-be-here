The concept

A movie where advertising becomes the plot.

Your Ad Could Be Here is an anti-film with no traditional story. Instead of characters, drama, and a normal plot, the film is built from paid placements, sponsor messages, brand appearances, scrolling text, credits, and absurd advertising moments.

If marketing can sell anything, can it sell a film that tells people not to watch it?
