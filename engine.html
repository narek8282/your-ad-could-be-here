Mathematical motion system.

The visual field uses parametric orbital equations, instanced 3D panels, shader glow, pointer deformation, and a 2D analytic overlay.

  radius = 4.5 + sin(i * 0.37) * 2.1

  modulate glow by marketNoise(t, i)
