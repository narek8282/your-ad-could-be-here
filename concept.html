Concept architecture

An anti-film where ads are not interruptions. They are the plot.

The project turns sponsor inventory into story structure: screen walls, ticker messages, credit injections, fake warnings, product ghosts, and paid pixels.

Phase 01

Seed brands

Invite a small group of Armenian and diaspora brands into the first visible layer.

Phase 02

Public market

Open a limited slot board where brands buy surfaces instead of normal ads.

Phase 03

Film assembly

The paid placements become scenes, transitions, credits, warnings, and visual noise.

Phase 04

Release loop

The campaign sells a film about the absurdity of selling attention.

Core question

If marketing can sell anything, can it sell a film that tells people not to watch it?

This is the cleanest version of the idea for US outreach: a viral advertising stunt by an Armenian creator, built as a cinematic system instead of a traditional pitch deck.
